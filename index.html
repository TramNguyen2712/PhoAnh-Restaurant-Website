    • Pho Anh is a Vietnamese restaurant located in Mankato, MN that is perfect for the whole family. We strive to serve our customers only the very best that Vietnamese cuisine has to offer. Set in a warm and friendly atmosphere, we offer friendly and attentive customer service, great prices, and—of course—delicious and authentic cuisine.

    Our Vietnamese Pho

    Our Vietnamese beef noodle soup is called pho. Cooked with beef bone, beef brisket, and herbs. The broth takes over 12 hours to reach the perfect flavor. Out pho is always served with green and white onion and a side of fresh beansprouts, thai basil, cilatro,, jalapenos, and lime
